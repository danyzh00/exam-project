Chipotle Sweet Potato Noodle Salad with Roasted Corn

INGREDIENTS

FOR THE SALAD

  • 1 brick of frozen udon
  • 1/2 cup kimchi, plus a bit of kimchi juice
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 sac of mentaiko
  • sliced green onions and nori, to finish
